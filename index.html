What's New

    All activities online!

    As the schools were shifting to online learning due to the COVID-19 pandemic, there was a tough time of thinking of how will a few activities be done online. However, Oberoi International School, with the cooperation with the whole school community, was able to conduct all acivities online.

    School not reopening anytime soon

    Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, OIS shifted to online learning. Now, almost one year after lockdown in India, the schools have still not reopened physically and high chances are that it will re-open in the next academic year starting in July, 2021.
